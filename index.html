About Douceur d’Airi


Tradition since 1889

Founded with a passion for refined flavors, our café is where Japanese elegance meets French artistry. Every dessert is a harmonious blend of delicate textures and exquisite taste, crafted to bring joy in every bite. We take pride in using only the finest seasonal ingredients — from Hokkaido milk to artisanal French chocolate — ensuring freshness, authenticity, and unmatched quality. Each creation reflects our belief that desserts are not just treats, but experiences to be savored.

At our café, sweetness is more than flavor — it’s a moment of happiness shared.
